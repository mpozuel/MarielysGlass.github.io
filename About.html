Mariely

Pictured here is Mariely and her dog, Pepe.

Mariely is an amatuer glass artist with the creative drive to succeed. Working along side artists with a combined 50 years experience, she is currently learning industry techniques and hope to master the art of glass. Her focus is primarily in the creation of glass jewelry and smoking accessories such as spoon pipes, bongs, and caps. Looking to place her foot in the door of the massive legal marijuana industry, she hopes to achieve a professional level of glass blowing and become a name in the glass industry.
