Our mission is to provide the public with a valuable tool that could be used to connect with a cultural organization offering specific services such as food, decor, clothing, etc. If you’re looking to “get in touch with your roots” Localito is the place to go. Our listings are comprised of hole-in-the-wall local shops that are family owned. We work together to bring you closer to understanding the world around you and its diverse offerings. We are a community-based platform and rely on user inputted data to help update our listings. Whether you’re ice fishing in the farthest corner of Alaska or sunbathing on the humid beaches of Florida you’re always close when home calls.
